Updates and progress on ultramap development.

Welcome to ultramap

October 19, 2025

ultramap is a desktop whiteboarding application that combines visual thinking with AI assistant integration. We're building a tool that stays out of your way while giving you powerful features when you need them.

Key features in early access:

  • Infinite canvas with sticky notes, shapes, frames, and connectors
  • Integration with Claude Desktop and VS Code via Model Context Protocol
  • Semantic search and knowledge graph for discovering connections
  • Local-first storage - your data stays on your machine
  • Currently available for Windows (macOS and Linux coming soon)

The v0.9.0 beta is completely free to download and use. The free beta will be available until the v1.0.0 release, at which point ultramap will be available as a one-time purchase with a lifetime license. Your feedback will help shape the future of ultramap.

More updates coming soon!
