How to Export

  • Option 1 (Figma/Sketch): Open ultramap-icon.svg in Figma or Sketch, then export at various sizes (1024px, 512px, 256px, etc.)
  • Option 2 (Browser): Right-click on any icon size above and "Save Image As" PNG
  • Option 3 (Command line): Use a tool like svg2png or ImageMagick to convert at different sizes
  • Option 4 (Online): Upload the SVG to CloudConvert or similar to export as PNG at 1024×1024
